About Us

We feel Pleasure to Introduce Ourselves as engineering Construction Company in the form of AK ENGINEERING CO. We have been serving in the field of execution of Ducting, Piping (fabrication & Erection), Heavy Structural (fabrication & Erection), Scaffolding, Blasting & Painting work as per client’s requirements / specifications.

MISSION AND VISION STATEMENT

AK ENGINEERING CO. will continue to play a major role in infrastructure development in INDIA. To help us achieve this aim, and grow our capacity and reach, we will be creating partnerships with further emerging contractors.

We shall be:

  • Customer focused
  • Leader & Involved team in the company driven initiatives for development.
  • Active for Continual Improvement
  • Fair and maintain beneficial supplier relationship

Quality Statement

AK ENGINEERING CO. provides quality infrastructure development in the form of mechanical works of refinery projects. Excellent working relationships have been established with other key players in the industry, which have yielded great rewards.

To be a Treasure for your organization by Appling Our Present capabilities:

  • Proper utilization of recourses provided by the company /Client
  • Taking necessary steps towards energy saving
  • Awareness training of employees
  • Constantly creating value addition to work assigned to us
  • Implementing systems and procedures to maintain standard of the Company

ENVIRONMENT POLICY

AK ENGINEERING CO. subscribe to the philosophy of retaining as much as possible of the natural flora and fauna. Clearance of sites is done with minimum damage to the environment. Where possible the natural flora or fauna is reinstated after completion of work.
