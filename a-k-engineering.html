ENVIRONMENT POLICY

AK ENGINEERING CO. subscribe to the philosophy of retaining as much as possible of the natural flora and fauna. Clearance of sites is done with minimum damage to the environment. Where possible the natural flora or fauna is reinstated after completion of work.
