About Us

BARNWAL BEVERAGES was established and registered in 2013 with its main office located in : Dhanbad, Jharkhand (INDIA)

Over the past seven years, BARNWAL BEVERAGES has accumulated a strong and healthy market trust through a simple principle that we follow: Earn Trust with Business BARNWAL BEVERAGES is among the leading Purifying and packaging of water in bottle and jar.

BARNWAL BEVERAGES has been providing support and services to all major city of Jharkhand state.

BARNWAL BEVERAGES is a manufacturer of all types of drinking water bottle and jar.
